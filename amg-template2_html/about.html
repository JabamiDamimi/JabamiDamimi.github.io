The Manufacturer of Medical Weight and Height Scale and Height Rods

Led lighting manufacturer in Taiwan - Pookoo Industrial Co.,Ltd. was founded in 1982 in Taipei, Taiwan. We diversified into LED lighting products in 2008 in response to rising eco-consciousness worldwide.

Pookoo provide Taiwan-made stylish led lamps including MR16 spotlights, E27 bulbs, PAR lamps, T8/T9 light tubes, light bars, downlights, floodlights, mood light.We offers you an extensive range of architectural lighting tools with a wide variety of lighting solutions for indoor and outdoor use。

 
  1. 01A complete product line of LED lighting products to meet various needs, from indoor lighting, outdoor lighting, decorative lighting to professional lighting.
  2. 02Backed by skilled, experienced R&D engineers and high quality LED chips from globally famous brands including CREE, Bridgelux, Lumileds, and Epistar, we offer lighting with the best functions in the market, including 850lm MR16 spotlight, 6400lm 4ft LED T8 light or 60000lm high bay light, etc.
  3. 03Our products come with Taiwan-made quality and are shipped with at least 2-year guarantee and the best after-sales service.
