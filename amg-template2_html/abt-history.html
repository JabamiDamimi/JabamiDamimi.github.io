  • History
  • 1982 Chi Chung was founded in Homei Township, Changhua County, Taiwan, and mainly produces lining fabric and cloth for footwear making.
  • 1989 Making fabric for illumination decoration and bag.
  • 1991 Starting the business of base fabrics for PU synthetic leather.
  • 1995 Manufacturing and selling curtain fabric, fabric of home decoration, fabric of training equipment and fabric of diving suit.
  • 1999 Specialized in the field of Microfiber and eyeglasses cleaning cloth.
  • 2001 Beginning the business of clean room wiper.
  • 2005 Developing functional fabrics such as moisture absorbing and quick drying, anti-bacterial and deodorant, UV cut and high stretch fabric.
