Company

The Manufacturer of Medical Weight and Height Scale and Height Rods Led lighting manufacturer in Taiwan - Pookoo Industrial Co.,Ltd. was founded in 1982 in Taipei, Taiwan. We diversified into LED lighting products in 2008 in response to rising eco-consciousness worldwide.
Backed by skilled, experienced R&D engineers and high quality LED chips from globally famous brands including CREE, Bridgelux, Lumileds, and Epistar, we offer lighting with the best functions in the market, including 850lm MR16 spotlight, 6400lm 4ft LED T8 light or 60000lm high bay light, etc.
